Registration is now open for the 14th annual JHMMC! The competition will take place October 11-12, 2025: Saturday for grades 4-6, Sunday for grades 7-8.
The 2025 JHMMC will be held across two days separated by grade (although each student only attends one of the days). This is different from the format of previous years.

Hosted at the Bergen County Academies

The BCA Math Team proudly presents the 14th Annual Joe Holbrook Memorial Math Competition, formerly known as the BCAMC, on October 11-12, 2025. This competition brings the best and brightest elementary and middle school students from the greater Bergen County area to meet and compete with fellow students.

To see more information about the Bergen County Academies Math Team, visit its official website at https://bcamathteam.org.
