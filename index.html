Hosted at the Bergen County Academies

The BCA Math Team proudly presents the 10th Annual Joe Holbrook Memorial Math Competition, formerly known as the BCAMC, on October 17, 2021. This competition brings the best and brightest elementary and middle school students from the greater Bergen County area to meet and compete with fellow students.

To see more information about the Bergen County Academies Math Team, visit its official website at https://bcamathteam.org.

This year for the first time, the BCA Math Team organized a second round: the JHIMC (Joe Holbrook Invitational Math Competition). This invitational round was for students who performed extremely well on the first round, and presented more challenging problems. Check out the results and problems for this round in the Archive. Over $2,000 of prizes were distributed to winners!
