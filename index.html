Hosted at the Bergen County Academies

The BCA Math Team proudly presents the 11th Annual Joe Holbrook Memorial Math Competition, formerly known as the BCAMC, on October 16, 2022. This competition brings the best and brightest elementary and middle school students from the greater Bergen County area to meet and compete with fellow students.

To see more information about the Bergen County Academies Math Team, visit its official website at https://bcamathteam.org.
